- T2: main=2, buffer=1

### Test: Slot Allocation After Build

**Case S1: Multiple T3 positions upgrade to T2**
- Initial: 3 positions in T3, each at ~10% (~10k each, total ~100k)
- Expected after autoRebalance: 2 in T2 main, 1 in T2 buffer

| Symbol | Initial Tier | Initial % | Expected Tier | Expected inBuffer |
|--------|-----------|---------|-------------|-----------------|
| GOOG | T3 | ~10% | T2 | false |
| AAPL | T3 | ~10% | T2 | false |
| NVDA | T3 | ~10% | T2 | true |

**Case S2: T3 positions cannot exceed T2 main limit**
- Initial: 3 positions, each at ~20% (upgrade to T2)
- Expected: Only 2 in T2 main, 1 stays in T3

**Case S3: First iteration uses target tier as old tier**
- Initial: position.tier=3 but value% = 20% (should be T2)
- Expected: stays at T2, not downgraded to T2
